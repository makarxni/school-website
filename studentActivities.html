The Four Houses

Every student belongs to one of four vibrant houses. Earn points, compete, and show your spirit!

Integrity

Color: Blue

Stand for truth. Integrity House leads with honesty, courage, and fairness.

Loyalty

Color: Yellow

Faithful and true. Loyalty House values friendship, support, and commitment.

Honesty

Color: Green

Open and sincere. Honesty House stands for truth and transparency in all things.

Unity

Color: Red

Together as one. Unity House brings everyone together to achieve greatness.
