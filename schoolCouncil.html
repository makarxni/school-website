Meet the Team

Who We Are

The Student Council at Dayspring International Academy is a dynamic team of elected student leaders dedicated to representing their peers, organizing school activities, and fostering a positive, inclusive school culture. Each member brings unique strengths, talents, and ideas to empower the student body and champion student voices.
