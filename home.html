About Our School

Dayspring International Academy (DIA) is a leading Christian international school established in January 2011, sister to Morning Glory International School (Abidjan). We run multiple campuses in Accra's East Legon–Adjiringanor area. Our mission: quality education for future leaders with a holistic, international curriculum — emphasizing not just academics, but wisdom, humility, compassion and character.

We nurture world-ready students by blending academic excellence with character formation. Our core values shape every student: Collaboration, Creativity, Innovation, Integrity, Care, Discipline, Excellence, Honesty, Loyalty.
