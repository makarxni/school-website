Our Admission Process

1

Inquiry & Application

Submit an online inquiry form or visit our campus. Complete the application form with required documents.

2

Assessment

Students may be required to take an assessment test depending on the grade level.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and parents will be invited for an interview with the admissions committee.

4

Admission Decision

Successful applicants will receive an offer letter. Complete the enrollment process by submitting required fees.
